Project Overview

About the Game

Space Dodger is a 2D arcade survival game developed using Unity. The player controls a spaceship and must survive for as long as possible while avoiding planets, meteors, and other obstacles throughout the space environment.

The game features a black hole gravity mechanic that affects the player's movement and adds an additional challenge to the gameplay. Players earn points as they survive, while the high-score system saves their best score between play sessions.

Key Features

Spaceship Controls

Player movement designed for navigating the spaceship through obstacles in the 2D space environment.

Black Hole Gravity

A black hole mechanic that applies gravitational force to the player's spaceship and influences its movement.

Dynamic Obstacles

Planets and meteors create hazards that the player must avoid while surviving in the game environment.

High Score System

A persistent high-score system using PlayerPrefs to save the player's best score between game sessions.

Score System

The player's score increases during gameplay, encouraging players to survive longer and improve their previous score.

PC & Android

The game was developed and built for both Windows PC and Android platforms.

Challenges & Solutions

01

Implementing Black Hole Gravity

Creating a black hole mechanic that could influence the spaceship's movement while still keeping the game controllable was one of the main gameplay challenges.

SOLUTION

I implemented a gravity-based mechanic that applies force toward the black hole, requiring the player to adjust their movement and avoid being pulled too close to it.

02

Balancing Obstacles & Gameplay

Placing and managing obstacles while keeping the gameplay challenging but playable required testing and adjustments.

SOLUTION

I adjusted the obstacle behavior and gameplay setup through repeated testing to create a better balance between difficulty and player movement.

03

Saving the High Score

The player's highest score needed to remain saved even after closing and restarting the game.

SOLUTION

I used Unity's PlayerPrefs system to store and retrieve the player's high score, allowing their best result to persist between game sessions.

06

What I Learned

Developing Space Dodger gave me hands-on experience with creating a complete 2D game in Unity using C#. Through this project, I improved my understanding of player movement, physics-based gameplay mechanics, collision handling, scoring systems, UI, and persistent data using PlayerPrefs.

Implementing the black hole gravity mechanic also helped me explore how physics can be used to create unique gameplay challenges. Building the game for both PC and Android gave me additional experience in adapting a project for different platforms and testing the overall gameplay experience.
